This 3-step tool makes it easy to contribute your photos and turn them into a collection of scientific observations.

In this first step, you can upload all your recent photos, which will be called pictures. You'll also be able to add information about the pictures after each is uploaded. These pictures will become part of a collection; you can add information about the collection later.

In the next step, you'll group your pictures into observations, because, for instance, you may have several photos of each species in each location.

The last step will allow you to add all sorts of information to the observations. You'll be able to add identifications - what species you think it is - and interactions between your observations and other species.

You can leave this Contribute process at any time, and your contributions will be saved. You can continue with this collection at any time by visiting My Contributions.
